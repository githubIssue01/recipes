Pasta cheese casserole

For 3-4 people

    Ingredience
  • Pasta 500 g
  • Gruyere 350 g
  • Cheddar 350 g
  • Milk 1 dl
  • Butter 100 g
  • Flour 3 EL
  • Breadcrumbs
    Steps
  1. Rasp cheese
  2. Make pasta
  3. Heat up butter in a pan, if its liquid add fleur and add gulp per gulp milk, wait for 3 min
  4. Add 1/2 cheese and stir, add spices
  5. Add pasta stir and put mass in oven dish
  6. Add Breadcrumbs and of the cheese on top
  7. Put it in oven for 30 min at 180 Â°C convection air
  8. Finito
